Kairouan

The spiritual capital of Tunisia, home to the Great Mosque and exquisite carpets.

About Kairouan

Kairouan is the fourth holiest city in Islam and a UNESCO World Heritage site. Its Great Mosque is a masterpiece of Islamic architecture. The city is also famous for its sweet 'Makroudh' pastries and world-renowned hand-knotted carpets.

Key Highlights

  • Great Mosque of Uqba
  • Medina of Kairouan
  • Aghlabid Basins
  • Carpet Workshops

Plan Your Visit

  • Best Time to Visit Spring (Mar-May) or Autumn (Sep-Nov)
  • Recommended Stay 2-3 Days
